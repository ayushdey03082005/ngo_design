About DonateChain

Revolutionizing charitable giving through blockchain technology

Our Mission

DonateChain is dedicated to creating a transparent, secure, and efficient ecosystem for charitable giving. We believe that transparency is the foundation of trust in philanthropy, and blockchain technology is the key to achieving this transparency at scale.

Every donation made through our platform is recorded on the blockchain, creating an immutable record that donors can verify. NGOs must provide detailed spending reports, which are stored on IPFS for permanent accessibility.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DonateChain.

How do I know my donation is safe? +

Your donations are processed via smart contracts on a blockchain, ensuring immutable and transparent records. We also verify all NGOs on our platform.

What are Soulbound NFTs? +

Soulbound NFTs (SBTs) are non-transferable digital tokens that serve as a permanent, verifiable record of your charitable contributions, acting as a digital badge of your generosity.

Why is spending data on IPFS? +

The InterPlanetary File System (IPFS) is a decentralized network for storing and sharing data. By storing spending reports on IPFS, we ensure that these documents are permanently accessible and resistant to censorship or alteration, further enhancing transparency.

Can I donate anonymously? +

Yes, you have the option to donate anonymously. While the transaction will still be recorded on the blockchain for transparency, your personal information or wallet address can be hidden from the public leaderboard.

Which NGOs are verified? +

All NGOs on DonateChain undergo a rigorous verification process to ensure their legitimacy, legal compliance, and commitment to their stated causes. You can find more details on our verification process in the About Us section.

What happens if an NGO misuses funds? +

Our platform's core is transparency. While we strive to onboard only legitimate organizations, the public nature of blockchain transactions and IPFS reports allows the community to monitor fund usage. In cases of confirmed misuse, we have protocols for delisting NGOs and working with relevant authorities.
